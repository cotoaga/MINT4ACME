2.4. Framework Structure

The MINT framework structure provides the technical foundation for implementing the business processes and objects described in the previous sections. It defines the architectural components, interfaces, and patterns used throughout the application.

This section is in development as part of the digital archaeology restoration project.

The complete content for Framework Structure will be added in a future update.

The MINT framework structure builds on the layered architecture approach described in the General Approach section, providing specific implementation details for each layer.

Continue to the next section on Benefits to understand the advantages of the MINT framework for ACME and its dealer network.
