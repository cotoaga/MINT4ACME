ACME-Vehicles has identified the need for an easy-to-use application that integrates the sales and information processes for sales consultants and customers. This application will act as a filter between the user and the existing subsystems, making the switch between subsystems transparent by providing a uniform look and feel.

The use of multimedia technology will provide a state-of-the-art interface that is both easy to use and enhancing the attractiveness of ACME and dealer products.

The main goal of MINT is to provide a solution for integrating the sales and information processes, that can be used as a showroom or as a public kiosk application by both customers and sales consultants. The application must be very flexible and allow ACME to control business rules. It also must support fast adaptation of branding and market positioning for different countries: ideally the system will react logically as an environment that integrates and extends existing sub-systems and behaves visually as a publishing and interaction medium for all kinds of users and places.

Even though today's customers are focused on multimedia and Internet technology by the mass media, it is important that the solution originate from key business requirements, not from flashy experimental technology. Only in that manner can a synergy of information gathering, production and sales be supported.
