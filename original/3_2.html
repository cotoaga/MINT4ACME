The choice of our browser-based MINT as the showroom application that will be rolled out to dealers across Europe will have major benefits for ACME-Vehicles.

The benefits will accrue from:

  • The unique, browser-based approach proposed by CSC PLOENZKE.
  • Our knowledge of the European automotive industry processes, in particular within the dealership. We gathered this knowledge in many client assignments for German, UK and US motor manufacturers.
  • Our competence in advanced image manipulation and enhancement and virtual reality. We have been involved in advanced graphics techniques for a very long time, mainly as part our work for NASA in connection with enhancement of space mission pictures (still and video) and for the US National Security Agency in connection with enhancement and interpretation of satellite pictures (still and video). The techniques that we developed in order to perform these tasks for our clients have found commercial applicability to other tasks, and as a consequence we have now established a very successful business in the provision of advanced graphics effects for the arcade game and games console markets. Currently we are starting to develop this business in the television and film industries .
  • Our competence in multimedia document management. CSC PLOENZKE has developed the multimedia document management system for the Deutsche Bibliothek in Frankfurt.
  • Our understanding of how a shrink-wrapped commercial product development differs from the development of an internal bespoke application. One of the very important lessons we have learned in the development of dealership systems is the perceived difference in the marketplace between application and product.

The experience gained in these activities will be made available to ACME for the development, testing, piloting and roll-out of MINT.
