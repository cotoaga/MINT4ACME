This section describes the main system elements of the MINT-Application, which form a client server architecture of interfacing layers. The first part of this section describes the layers, their main functions, the interfaces between the layers and the data stored within them.

The second part outlines the design of the requested MINT versions:

  • Dealer-MINT Standard
  • Dealer-MINT Laptop (online & off-line),
  • Kiosk-MINT (public areas or dealer showroom).

Following these two parts are others describing further objects, processes, details and elements:

  • Multimedia Objects (MMOs)
  • Help System and Documentation

The goal of this section is to provide the necessary background to understand the performance, the flexibility and the advantages of the proposed MINT technical solution.
